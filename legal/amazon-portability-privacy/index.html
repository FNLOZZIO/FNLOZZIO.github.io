Amazon Data Portability Privacy Notice

This notice explains how data received through Amazon Data Portability is handled by FNLOZZIO.

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Who operates this integration

This integration is operated by FNLOZZIO for user-authorized personal use. It is not offered as a public service.

What data may be accessed

Subject to the granted Amazon consent scopes, the integration may access order history, returns, refunds, and related order metadata.

Why the data is used

The data is used only to import, review, and retain the authorized records for personal use.

How the data is stored

Imported data is stored under the operator's control and used only for the purposes described in this notice. Access is limited to the operator.

Sharing and sale of data

Data accessed through this integration is not sold. It is not disclosed to third parties except where required by law.

Retention

Data may be retained as part of personal records until it is deleted by the operator or access is revoked.

Revocation and deletion

Consent can be revoked through Amazon's authorization controls. Once revoked, no new data will be imported through Amazon Data Portability until authorization is granted again. Existing locally stored records may also be deleted by the operator.

Contact

For questions about this notice, use the public GitHub profile: github.com/FNLOZZIO
